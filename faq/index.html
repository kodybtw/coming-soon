FAQ

Everything about Open In 2050

1. What is Open In 2050? ⌄
Open In 2050 is a permanent digital grid consisting of exactly 900 slots. Each slot is a personal time capsule that you seal with your content (text, photo, video, audio, or confession). No one — not even you — can open it until January 1, 2050.
2. When does it launch and how many slots are available? ⌄
The project launches on April 27, 2026. There are exactly 900 slots. Once they’re gone, they’re gone forever.
3. What can I put inside my slot? ⌄
Anything: letters, photos, videos, voice messages, ideas, confessions or memories.
4. Can I edit after sealing? ⌄
No. Once sealed, it cannot be edited or viewed until 2050.
5. Is my content private? ⌄
Yes, only minimal preview is visible. Full content is hidden until 2050.
6. What happens in 2050? ⌄
The entire grid opens publicly at the same time.
7. How much does a slot cost? ⌄
Price will be announced on launch day.
8. Can I buy multiple slots? ⌄
Yes, multiple slots are allowed.
9. Is this permanent? ⌄
Yes, we are building long-term storage systems for durability.
10. Will more slots be added? ⌄
No, only 900 will ever exist.
11. How will I be notified in 2050? ⌄
Via email and possibly a public event.
